**Hack Club** ([hackclub.com](https://hackclub.com)) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499), founded in 2014, whose mission is to foster a wholesome generation of programmers, technologists, founders, and engineers. At Hack Club, young people build the agency, network, and technical talent to think big and do big things in the world.

**Hack Club Fallout** is an online program followed by an in-person hackathon summit. In the online portion, students design and build hardware projects, individually or in groups. We'll provide funding for parts and electronics, and they'll join a community of thousands of likeminded teenagers doing the same. After 60 hours of building, they qualify for a 7-day experience in Shenzhen, China.

We understand that being a parent of a Hack Clubber is not easy. Asking for permission to go to a completely different country is a hard thing for them to do, and also very intimidating for parents.

We want to do everything we can to help you feel more comfortable. **Hack Club operates on the principle of radical transparency** and we promise to communicate with you frequently and transparently.

**We've written a [Parents' Guide and Youth Protection Plan](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dXDIBm7SWui5rbK3zh7188UmLC0cv_dsMj7ynr3POho/edit?tab=t.q4hvz46um9np) to help you understand how Hack Club, and this event, operates.**

If you have more questions, [book a 1-on-1 call with Johnathan](https://hack.club/fallout-parent-call), or reach out to us via email: fallout@hackclub.com.
